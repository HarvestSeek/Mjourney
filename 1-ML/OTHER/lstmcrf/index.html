    1. 1

    1. 基本定义

    • 假设序列长度为 $m$，$Y$ 的取值范围 $\lbrace 1,...,n \rbrace$
    • LSTM 和 CRF 的时间步相互对应，LSTM 每个时间步的 $n$ 个输出中，最大值作为 CRF $Y_i$ 的状态特征，相应的标记值作为 $Y_i$ 的标记
    • CRF 只有一个转移特征函数和一个状态特征函数，且都不考虑位置变量，可以用矩阵表示
      • $T_k = [t(Y_{k}=i, Y_{k+1}=j)]$: 位置 $k$ 处 各个标记间的转移关系 / 函数
          $\displaystyle T_k = \left(\begin{array}{lll} t_{11} & t_{12} & t_{13} \\ t_{21} & t_{22} & t_{23} \\ t_{31} & t_{32} & t_{33} \end{array}\right) $
      • $S_k = (s(Y_k=1), ..., s(Y_k=3))$: 位置 $k$ 处 各个标记的状态特征

    损失函数:

      $\displaystyle P(Y|X) = \frac{1}{Z} \exp\left(score(X, Y)\right) = \frac{1}{Z} \exp\left(\sum_{i=2}^n t(Y_{i-1}, Y_i) + \sum_{i=1}^n s(Y_i)\right) $
       $\displaystyle \mathrm{Loss} = - \log P(Y|X) = \log Z - score(X, Y)$

    2. 计算 $\log Z$

    由 $\displaystyle Z = \sum_Y \prod_C \psi(Y_C) = \sum_Y \exp\left(\sum_{i=2}^n t(Y_{i-1}, Y_i) + \sum_{i=1}^n s(Y_i)\right)$，
    定义 $\alpha(p, q) = $ 截至位置 $p$ 以标记值 $q$ 结尾的路径和取 $\log$，因此

       $ \begin{aligned}\displaystyle \alpha(p, q) &= \log \sum_{Y_1, ...,Y_{p-1}} \exp\bigg(score(\mathrm{start}\to Y_{p}=q)\bigg) \\ &= \log \sum_{Y_1, ...,Y_{p-1}} \exp\bigg(score(\mathrm{start}\to Y_{p-1})+ t(Y_{p-1}, Y_p=q) + s(Y_p=q)\bigg) \\ &= \log \sum_{Y_{p-1}=k} \exp\bigg( \alpha(p-1, k) + t(Y_{p-1}=k, Y_p=q) + s(Y_p=q) \bigg) \end{aligned} $
    $\mathrm{Note}$:
    $(1).\ \displaystyle \exp\left(\alpha(p-1, k)\right) = \sum_{Y_1, ..., Y_{p-2}} \exp \left( score(\mathrm{start} \to Y_{p-1}=k)\right)$
    $\displaystyle \begin{aligned}(2).\ \alpha(0, q) &= \log \sum \exp \left( score(Y_0 = q) \right) \\ & = \begin{cases} 0 & \text{if }q=\text{start}, \log(1)=0 \\ -10000 & \text{else, instead of }\log(0) = - \infty \end{cases} \end{aligned} $

    具体的计算过程为:

    • $(1).$ 初始化:
      $\displaystyle \alpha_0 = (\alpha_{00},\ \alpha_{01},\ ... ,\alpha_{0n})$
    • $(2).$ 递推:
        $\mathsf{a.}$ 对于时间步 $k$，可知:
      $\displaystyle \alpha_k = (\alpha_{k0},\ \alpha_{k1},\ ... ,\alpha_{kn}) \xrightarrow[]{\text{broadcast by column}} \left(\begin{array}{lll} \alpha_{k0} & \alpha_{k0} & \cdots & \alpha_{k0} \\ \alpha_{k1} & \alpha_{k1} & \cdots & \alpha_{k1} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ \alpha_{kn} & \alpha_{kn} & \cdots & \alpha_{kn} \end{array}\right) $
      $\displaystyle T_k = \left(\begin{array}{lll} t_{00} & t_{01} & \cdots & t_{0n} \\ t_{10} & t_{11} & \cdots & t_{1n} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ t_{n0} & t_{n1} & \cdots & t_{nn} \end{array}\right) $
      $\displaystyle S_k = (s(Y_k=1),\ ..., s(Y_k=n)) \xrightarrow[]{\text{broadcast by row}} \left(\begin{array}{lll} s_{k1} & s_{k2} &\cdots & s_{kn} \\ s_{k1} & s_{k2} &\cdots & s_{kn} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ s_{k1} & s_{k2} &\cdots & s_{kn} \end{array}\right) $
        $\mathsf{b.}$ 三者相加:
      $ \begin{aligned} R &= \alpha_{k} + T_k + S_k \\ &= \left(\begin{array}{lll} \alpha_{k0}+t_{00}+s_{k0} & \alpha_{k0}+t_{01}+s_{k1} & \cdots & \alpha_{k0}+t_{0n}+s_{kn} \\ \alpha_{k1}+t_{10}+s_{k0} & \alpha_{k1}+t_{11}+s_{k1} & \cdots & \alpha_{k1}+t_{1n}+s_{kn} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ \alpha_{kn}+t_{n0}+s_{k0} & \alpha_{kn}+t_{n1}+s_{k1} & \cdots & \alpha_{kn}+t_{nn}+s_{kn} \end{array}\right) \end{aligned} $
        $\mathsf{c.}$ 对 $R$ 矩阵按列求 $\text{log\verb|-|sum\verb|-|exp}$:
      $\alpha_{k+1} = (\alpha_{k+1,0},\ \alpha_{k+1,1},\ ... ,\alpha_{k+1,n})$
    • $(3).$ 终止:
      $\displaystyle \text{result} = \sum_{i=1}^n \alpha_{mi}$

    $2.\ \mathsf{Viterbi:}$

    $ \begin{aligned} \delta(k, j) &=score(Y_0,Y_1,...,Y_k=j) \\ &= \max_{l} { \delta(k-1, l) + t(Y_{k-1}=l, Y_k=j) + s(Y_k=j)} \end{aligned} $
